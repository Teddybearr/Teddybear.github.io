DateIdeas is a growing startup founded by one of my seniors. The company provides interesting and unique leisure activity recommendations to youths aged 16 to 26 in a convenient and personalized manner. Before I joined, they only had one software developer and they were in the midst of a website revamp. It was quite a daunting task considering this was my first internship and the only prior experience I had was my Orbital Project (PlenR).

I worked closely with the designer, picking up new tools and technologies along the way. To view the fruits of my labor, do visit our newly revamped website here: https://dateideassg.com/
